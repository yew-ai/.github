# 🌳 Yew AI

**Building social simulators with language model agents.**

Yew AI develops tools, models, and research artifacts that let scholars and policymakers simulate human-scale social phenomena _in silico_ — before they unfold in the real world.

## What we work on

- **Multi-agent LLM simulation frameworks** — open source, reproducible
- **Computational social science** — replications, novel experiments
- **Educational tooling** — making social simulation accessible to undergrads and researchers
- **Open research** — papers, datasets, weights, all in the open

## Status

We're in seed mode — no product yet, lots of curiosity. Watch this space.
